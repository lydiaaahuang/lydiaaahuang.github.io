Spencer Reid

Reid is my favourite BAU team member. A genius who struggles in social situations but an absolute sweetheart. He is and incredible asset to the team due to his eidetic memory and the fact he can read 20,000 words per minute. His childhood is riddled with childhood bullying and having to be raised by his single mother dealing with paranoid schizophrenia. Heartbreakingly at the age of 18 Reid had to send his mother to a mental institution. Throughout the show Reid is worried that his mother's illness can be passed down genetically. Reid is often seen struggling with mental health and drug addiction after being held hostage by serial killer Tobias Hankel (S02E36.) Furthermore everyone who Reid's ever loved has left him constantly. His mother who he looses as her schizophrenia gets worse and worse and ends up forgetting him. JJ who falls in love with Will and Maeve his love dying. Gideon his mentor leaves him with a note, Morgan who has taken Reid under his wing leaves.

Although Reid's been through so much he stays true to himself. He's still the adorable genius whi has trouble with social behaviour but tries his best.

Honestly Reid deserved better. He was in love with JJ and then he found Maeve who got murdered in front of him. He's been through so much and he deserved a happier ending TT. I would sell my kidney for Reid's happiness because he deserves it.
