About The Show

Criminal Minds is an American thriller, mystery and crime drama. It revolves around the
methodology and psychosis of criminals. The show is produced by Jeff Davis and
first aired in 2005 and lasted till 2020. It ran for a total of 15 seasons consisting of 324 episodes.
Primarily the show follows a team of specialists from the FBI known as
the BAU (behaviourial analysis unit.)

Throughout the 15 seasons the main members of the group Aaron Hotchner/Hotch (Thomas Gibson),
Derek Morgan (Shemar Moore), Spencer Reid (Matthew Gray Gubler), Jennifer Jareau (A. J. Cook),
Penelope Garcia (Kirsten Vangsness), Emily Prentiss (Paget Brewster) and David Rossi (Joe Mantegna)
work as a team to solve crimes. However the cast changes up a lot and brings back old characters frequently.

Criminal Minds became one of the top watched shows on the CBS in America leading to a number of
spin offs including Criminal Minds: Beyond Borders, Criminal Minds: Suspect Behaviour
and even a Korean version of Criminal Minds. Surprisingly the spin offs did not do as well
and had low view counts. Beyond Borders only made it up to season 2 whilst
Suspect Behaviour never made it to the season 1 finale.

About Me



Hi, my name is Lydia and I am a Student at ANU. From a young
age I have been obsessed with fiction. Fictional books, tv shows,
characters. Although as I have grown I have developed an interest
in crime dramas and have proudly watched most of the infamous crime
shows such as NCIS (which i didn't enjoy) Law and Order (original and
SVU), bones etc. So I believe I have a fair amount of knowledge on
crime shows and plots.

Although watching so many crime shows you can see a few similar
plot lines and the roles of the team are alike (nerd, hunk, beauty,
comical relief.) The first time I watched an episode of a crime show
was at the ripe age of 8 when I walked in on my Aunty watching an episode
of Criminal Minds. However back then I was easily frightened and never
made it through the whole episode but now I am a part of the criminal minds
fandom. Even though Criminal Minds changes characters often they still create
an emotional connection to the main team. Through the 15 seasons I have
become attached to the BAU team and loved watching the growth and
development of characters.

Personally I am a Reid and JJ shipper and (spoiler alert) season 15 ending with
JJ confessing she has been in love with Reid all these years really made me
extremely frustrated at the show (why did they bring up the abandoned love
plot between JJ and Reid ON THE FINAL EPISODE.)

Because of my love for Criminal Minds I have decided to make a website
all about the show.

About The Website


Criminal Minds is a fandom page on the hit tv show Criminal Minds. Unlike most available Criminal Mind
fandom pages this websites focuses more on my personal opinions of the shows and things that were
hit and a miss. Plot lines that were messy or characters that should or shouldn't have been killed off.
This blog talks about topics that you would talk about with your friends when discussing the show.
Basically my opinion on various aspects of Criminal Minds.
